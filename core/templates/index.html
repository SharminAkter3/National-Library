How do I borrow books from the library?

To borrow books from the library, you can visit our physical location or access our online catalog. Search for the book you want, and if it's available, you can check it out using your library card. Remember to return the book by the due date to avoid late fees.

How can I renew my library books?

You can renew your library books online through our website or by visiting the library in person. Log in to your library account, navigate to your checked-out items, and select the option to renew. Please note that renewal options may vary depending on the library's policies.

How do I access online library resources?

Accessing online library resources is easy. Simply log in to your library account on our website, navigate to the digital library section, and explore the available resources such as e-books, audiobooks, and online databases. You may need to use your library card credentials to access certain resources.

What if the book I want is not available?

If the book you want is not available, you can place a hold on it through our online catalog. When the book becomes available, we'll notify you, and you can pick it up from the library. Additionally, you can request an interlibrary loan to borrow the book from another library within our network.

How can I donate books to the library?

We welcome book donations to enrich our collection and support our community. You can drop off your donated books at our library during operating hours. Alternatively, you can contact us to arrange a donation pickup or inquire about our donation guidelines and criteria.
